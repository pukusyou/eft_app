Description

This site is for identifying items needed for the "Escape from Tarkov" task and hideout.

Please see below for details on how to use the system.

Treatment

  1. Click on "Task" and "Hideout" to see the list.
  2. Select the "Task" and "Hideout Level" that is being completed and click the "Decide" button.
  3. The required items are displayed.
  4. By clicking on "Select," you can decide for yourself which items to display.
  5. Click "Share" to share the check status at that point.

※In the task selection screen, pressing the "✓" mark selects all tasks for the corresponding dealer.

※On the item list screen, you can switch the items to be displayed by pressing the "Display Settings" button.
